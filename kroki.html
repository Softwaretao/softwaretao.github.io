Kroki Macro for Confluence


A macro which integrates Kroki.io on Confluence page.

Please make sure that Kroki.io can be accessed from your Confluence server. You can Try syntax on Kroki.io.

This Macro can help you to paste URL to embed remote images into your Confluence with:

  • Image URL

This Macro can also help you to create a wide variety of diagrams from textual descriptions:

  • BlockDiag (BlockDiag, SeqDiag, ActDiag, NwDiag, PacketDiag, RackDiag)
  • BPMN
  • Bytefield
  • C4 (with PlantUML)
  • Ditaa
  • Erd
  • Excalidraw
  • GraphViz
  • Mermaid
  • Nomnoml
  • Pikchr
  • PlantUML
  • SvgBob
  • UMLet
  • Vega
  • Vega-Lite
  • WaveDrom
  • ......
