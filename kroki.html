Kroki Macro for Confluence (Cloud)

Kroki Macro for Confluence (Data Center)

A macro which integrates Kroki.io on Confluence page.

Please make sure that Kroki.io can be accessed from your Confluence server. You can Try syntax on Kroki.io.

If your Confluence Data Center cannot connect to kroki.io, you can configure a custom kroki server.
