General Privacy and Data Collection Policy of Softwaretao

Cloud Deployment

In Cloud, XDevPod app doesn't access, store, or otherwise process any personal data or table content with the exception of the cases described below.

For customer UI apps, all the macros are embedded as iframes on Confluence pages. An iframe receives data inserted in the macro body directly from Confluence without going through our server.

Data Storage

We do not store customer data on our servers.

Changes

XDevPod may periodically update this policy.
