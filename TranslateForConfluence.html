Translate for Confluence


We built this App on the Atlassian-hosted Forge platform so you can enjoy highest levels of security and performance.

This app helps your foreign language readers understand what you are publishing.
It's easy to use, doesn't require any settings from the publisher, and readers can get the translation results quickly.

Steps for readers to get translation results

  • Step1: Select Text

    • Select some text you want to translate when viewing the Confluence page.

  • Step2: Click Translate Button

    • Click the 'Translate' item under the dropdown menu that appears above the selected text.

  • Step3: View Translation Results

    • Read the quick translation results, or you can click on the link at the top to go to the translation site for more information.
